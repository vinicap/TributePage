Thomas à Kempis
Following Christ steps in modern times
Here are some quotes from Imitation of Christ:
  • “All men desire peace, but very few desire those things that make for peace.”
  • “Fight like a man. Habit is overcome by habit.”
  • “All that is in the world is vanity except to love God and serve him only.”
  • “It is not really a small thing when in small things we resist self.”
  • “Let temporal things be in the use, eternal things in the desire.”
  • “It is vanity, too, to covet honours, and to lift up ourselves on high...It is vanity, to love that which quickly passeth away and not to hasten
    where eternal joy abideth.”
  • "Whoever wishes to understand fully the words of Christ must try to pattern his whole life on that of Christ."
  • "Every man naturally desires knowledge; but what good is knowledge without fear of God? Indeed a humble rustic who serves God is better
    than a proud intellectual who neglects his soul to study the course of the stars."
  • "If I knew all things in the world and had not charity, what would it profit me before God Who will judge me by my deeds?"

"Remarkable for its simple language and style, it emphasizes the spiritual rather than the materialistic life, affirms the rewards of being Christ-centred, and supports Communion as a means to strengthen faith."
